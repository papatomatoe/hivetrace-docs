# Guardrails and policies

## When to use guardrails

Guardrails provide a baseline protection layer. In most setups, you enable them for every application as a default safety baseline.

## When you need custom policies

Custom policies encode **domain constraints** (what is allowed vs disallowed) and compliance requirements specific to your business.
